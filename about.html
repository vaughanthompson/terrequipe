About Terrequipe

We are your local construction, earthmoving and agricultural machinery dealers. Since our inception over ten years ago, Terrequipe has attracted passionate and knowledgeable talent to our organisation. This talent has been the fuel behind the rapid growth of our expertise and product offerings that are beyond an organisation of our years. If you are doing business in Central Queensland, our teams in Rockhampton and Mackay have your back.

Humble beginnings...

From our home base in Rockhampton and drawing off the strength and experience of parent company Transdiesel Limited, New Zealand’s premier national construction equipment distributor, Terrequipe have been on a mission to provide world-class machinery and support to our local communities. The main force behind this mission has been Greg Landsberg and Matt Coolee, our General Manager and Business Development Manager.

Greg and Matt, together with their team, have turned Terrequipe into what it is today by using their lifetime of knowledge and industry experience to build strong, trusted relationships with suppliers and customers.

... to best in show!

Terrequipe’s focus on our team and their families means we are also committed to our local community. This commitment to our community ensures we have our eye on the future for possible opportunities to improve our operations and organisation.

This commitment also means the team at Terrequipe are constantly working. Working on new ways to maximise returns for our customers with the best equipment and support available.

JCB Dealership

Having been the authorised JCB dealer for Central Queensland for over ten years, we are the regions experts on JCB machinery. Since establishment in 1945, JCB have built their reputation by pioneering new machinery, such as their telehandlers and backhoe loaders.

This focus on continual improvement has placed them as one of the leaders in this field. JCB also offer a wide range of excavators, skid-steers, generators, and more.
