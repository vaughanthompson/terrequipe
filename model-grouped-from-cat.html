MF 8700 S

The MF 8700 S Series is aimed at customers looking for a New touch of high horse power and a precision farming tractor with inspired design and optimised efficiency.

The MF 8700 S with its powerful new styling is designed to captivate you from the start. The NEW MF light bar and iconic MF triple triangle logo is at the heart of the ‘S’ effect distinctive styling, the full embodiment of MF’s power, design and engineering quality.

Overview

The on and off-board technology solutions incorporated into the MF 8700 S Series are a clear indication of the pace of progress and innovation setting by Massey Ferguson. Information is power when it comes to get the job done more efficiently and more productively.

This is the reason why, Massey Ferguson delivers straightforward and dependable technology solutions resulting in unbeatable ease of use, improved productivity and profitability through higher yields, lower inputs costs and greater profits.

  • 8.4 litre AGCO power 6 cylinder engine
  • Stepless precision
  • Cruise control
  • Multipad joystick MF 8700 S exclusive models
  • Minimum weight of 10.8 T
  • Continuously-variable Dyna-VT transmission
  • Dynamic Tractor Management (DTM) automatically controls engine and transmission
  • Engine power management 30 hp boost
More power efficiency brings more traction

Up to 370hp - The most powerful tractors ever built by Massey Ferguson, delivering the ultimate output with low running costs.

Efficient driveline boosts productivity

HDyna-VT transmission with Engine Power Management delivers more power when it is needed most. Perfect engine and transmission combination - matched to deliver maximum output.

Choose the correct tyre to protect your precious soil

2.15m rear wheel diameter enhances traction, reduces soil compaction and maximises performance. Michelin AxioBib 2 tyre option puts more lugs in contact with the soil, lengthening the footprint by 26% and improving traction by up to 28%.

MF Technology at your fingertips

MF Datatronic 5 touchscreen terminal: Advanced and intuitive. MF Guide: Submetre to centimetre precision from a wide choice of correction signals including Trimble and Novatel receivers. MF ISOBUS with Multipad: Efficient and comfortable to use.

Ultimate comfort and safety for a more productive working day

Large, quiet, four-post cab offers 360° visibility.
